Controls:

  • Draw: Click
  • Erase: Right Click
  • Change Tile: Q/E or L/R Arrows
  • Move Camera: WASD
  • Zoom Camera: + / -
  • Music & SFX Options: O
  • Hide UI: H

The game auto-saves every five seconds.
